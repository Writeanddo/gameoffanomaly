Cyberpunk 3rd person shooter - investigate anomaly within NeuroScape!

Controls

game requires keyboard and mouse to play

  • WASD - move
  • Space - jump
  • E - Interact
  • LMB - Fire
  • RMB - Aim
  • Q - Scanner
  • Shift - Dash
  • Esc, P - pause/menu
